About Us

This website is made for the sole purpose of helping and teaching the beginners. By using this website, we asure every people who visit here will have a grasp of knowledge of the use of css and its fundamentals and how to use and utilize it to fit the style of the said user. Please be advised that we intend no harm to everyone and that our mission is just to help people learn new set of skills to help them in whatever their purpose of learning.

Our Mission

We created this website to help people understand the fundamentals of CSS or Cascading Style Sheet. We want to share all basic things of css to every people that want to learn this skill as it may help them in the career path that they take in web developing.

Our Vision

Seeing people getting satisfied to what they learn is what warms our heart. And by building this website, We hope that every people who visit here will have that satisfaction in their face and can help them in their success.
